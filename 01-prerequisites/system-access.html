System Access

SAP BTP Subaccount

For BTP-connected development you need access to a BTP subaccount. Contact your project lead or SAP basis team to get:

  • DEV space access in the relevant Cloud Foundry org
  • S/4HANA sandbox credentials for API testing
  • Destination Service configuration rights

Verify your CF login works:

cf login -a https://api.cf.eu10.hana.ondemand.com
# Enter your SAP BTP email + password when prompted
cf target   # Should show your org and space

SAP BTP Trial (Self-Study)

For self-study and exercises without a project system, create a free BTP trial account:

  1. Go to sap.com/trial
  2. Register with your business email
  3. Choose the US East (VA) or Europe (Frankfurt) region
  4. Activate the Business Application Studio service
Trial limitations: Trial accounts expire after 90 days of inactivity and have limited resources. Use your project subaccount for real work.
